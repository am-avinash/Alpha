Frequently Asked Questions

Can I join now?

Yes, you can join now as this is a self-paced course. All Lectures will be provided on Day1.

Can I do this course with my college classes?

Yes, it is a self-paced course and can be done along with college classes.

Is the course in Hindi or English?

The course is taught in Hinglish (a mix of Hindi & English).

How long is the course access?

The course is accessible for the duration of one year.

Will there be a Certificate of completion?

Yes, you will get a certificate after finishing this course.

Is there a course schedule?

All chapters will be unlocked on Day1. We advise you to fix a time for yourself (eg - 9PM) & study according to your schedule.

Will there be question practice in the course?

Yes, the course has 200+ carefully curated questions solved in class.

Will there be projects in the course?

Yes, the course will have many minor & major projects that will help to strengthen your concepts and you can mention in your resume/portfolio.

What is MERN Stack in Development?

MERN stands for MongoDB, Express, React & Node. These are the 4 key technologies that will be covered in the batch. MERN is the most popular stack for web development & choice for today’s web developers.

What is Web Development?

It is the process of building websites for the internet. We use many websites like Amazon, Flipkart, Netflix & many more. We will learn the process of designing, building & deploying such websites on the internet.
